Our Story

Clippy was born from a simple observation: while everyone was talking about AI, few were building it in ways that truly transformed businesses. We saw companies struggling with AI projects that were either too complex, too expensive, or too disconnected from real business needs.

In 2020, our founders—a team of AI researchers, software engineers, and product designers—came together with a shared vision: to make intelligent technology accessible, practical, and impactful for businesses of all sizes.

We started by helping small startups implement AI features that gave them competitive advantages. As word spread, we grew into a full-service AI agency, working with healthcare providers, retailers, manufacturers, and educational institutions to transform their operations with intelligent solutions.

Today, we're proud to have delivered over 50 AI projects, generating millions in value for our clients while staying true to our core belief: technology should work for people, not the other way around.
