Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start a project?

Most projects begin within 2-3 weeks of proposal acceptance. For urgent needs, we offer expedited startup options.

What's your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary by project complexity. AI Discovery Sprints take 2 weeks, while full builds typically range from 8-16 weeks.

Do you work with startups?

Yes! We work with businesses of all sizes. For startups, we offer flexible engagement models and often begin with AI Discovery Sprints.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have deep experience in healthcare, retail, manufacturing, finance, and education, but our AI-first approach applies across industries.

How do you ensure data privacy and security?

We follow industry best practices, implement robust security measures, and can work with your existing compliance frameworks.

What happens after project completion?

We provide ongoing support, monitoring, and optimization options. Most clients continue with our AI Transformation package.
